Cessna 337 Skymaster (Thranda/XP12)
--Preflight--
Wght/Bal=Complete
Tie-Downs/Covers/Chocks=Removed
Docs/Charts/NOTAMS=Secured
--Before Engine Start--
Batt/Altr=On
Volt Reg=As desired
Nav Lht=On
L/G=Dwn, dwn lht illum, tst horn/lht
Cowl Flaps=Open
Fuel=Front left main, rear right main
Radios & Elect Equip=Off
--Front Engine Start--
Beacon=On
Pwr=Mix rich, prop high rpm, throttle 1in
Pump=On
Fuel Flow live=Ign start, pump off
Oil Press=Green arc in 30 seconds
Front Egn OK=Repeat rear egn (delay @ hot/high fields)
--Before Taxi--
Pwr=Throttle idle, prop low rpm, mix lean
Avionics=On
Flt Plan/Altimeter/DG or HSI/Hdg Indic/Nav & Comm Radios=Set
Xpndr=STBY, set Squawk
Taxi Lhts=On
--Taxi Out (if loose gravel/FOD, consider rear-egn taxi)--
Xpndr=On
Mag Comp=Swings freely
Wing Flaps=Set
Att Indic=Stable
Hdg Indic=Correct, verify mag compass
Turn Rate Indic & Slip/Skid=Correct
--Run Up--
Prkg Brk=Set
Rear Egn=Chkd green ranges
Flt Cntrls=Free/Correct
Elev/Rud Trim=Set T/O
Pwr=Mix rich, prop high rpm, throttles 1800rpm
Mags=Check, 50 RPM max drop diff
Props=Check feather, return high RPM
Alts/Egn Instr=Check, suction guage 5.0-5.4in/hg
Throttles=Check idle
Door/Windows=Closed/As desired
Prkg Brk=Release
--Normal Take-Off--
Ext Lhts=Strobe/Lndg on, taxi off
Xpndr=ALT, begin flight time
Pwr=Mix rich/high rpm/full throttle
Egn Instr=Both egns live
--Gravel/Cinder Field Take-Off--
Ext Lhts=Strobe/Lndg on, taxi off
Xpndr=ALT, begin flight time
Mixtures=Rich
Pwr=Full throttle & 2800rpm rear-egn
IAS Live=Full pwr/rpm front-egn
--Climb--
Wing Flaps=Retract
Strobes=Off
Landing Gear=Retract clear 1000ft
Cowl Flaps=As req (monitor cyl head temps)
--Cruising--
Cowl Flaps=Closed
DG/HSI=Periodically confirm set to magnetic compass (level flight only)
Instr=Set appro
--Descent--
Cold Weather=Observe temps, keep pwr green
Mix=Enrichen as req
--Before Landing--
Wing Flaps=Set
L/G=Dwn, lht green, check handle return neutral
Strobes=On
Cowl Flaps=Open
Pwr=Mix rich, props high rpm
--Taxi In--
Wing Flaps=Retract
Ext Lhts=Strobe/Lndg off, taxi on
Xpndr=On, end flight time
Elev/Rud Trim=Set t/o
--Securing aircraft--
Prkg Brk=Set
Radios/Elec Equip=Off
Pwr=Throttles idle, props feather mix cut-off
Fuel Sels/Mags=Front/Rear Off
Nav/Beacon Lhts=Off
Starter/Master/Altr=Off
--Checklist by Lightdasher360, thanks to docs by Thranda for X-Plane, official Super SkyMaster Owner's Manual by Cessna, and various pilots sharing exp & procs online!--
