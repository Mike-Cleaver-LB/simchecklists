Lancair Legacy (Aerobask/XP)
--Before Start--
Wght & Bal, Oxy, Maint=Chkd
Docs/Charts/NOTAMS=Secured
Covers/Chocks=Removed
Brkrs=In
Brks=Check & hold
Batt/Alt/Avionics=Off
L/G=Dwn
Nav Lht, GPU=On, as req
--Starting--
Batt charged=Batt on, GPU/Alt Air off
Fuel=Check, L/R
Pwr=Mix rich, prop full, throttle 1/4"
Pump=Check, prime as req
Prop Clear=Set mags, start
Pwr=RPM as req (1000 cold/800 warm), lean mix
Oil Press Green=Alt/Elec Trim/Avionics/Radios on
--Pre-Taxi--
Flt Plan/Altimeters/Radios=Set
Xpdr=Sby, set squawk
--Taxi--
Canopy=Latched, door seal on
Hdg=Rwy
Xpdr, Taxi Lht=On
Instr=Check
Flaps/Trim=Set
--Run Up--
Prkg Brk=Set
CHT/Oil Temp=Green
Pwr=Mix rich, throttle 1800rpm
Prop=Cycle twice
Mags=Check, max 150 drop & 50 diff
Pwr=Throttle check full dwn then idle, lean mix
--Before Takeoff--
Hdg=Set initial
Instr=Chkd
Oil Temp & CHT=100f/38c & 150f/65c min
AP/FD=Off/As desired
Cntrls=Free/Correct
--Takeoff--
Lndg/Strobes=On
Xpdr=Alt
Time Off=Note
Pwr=Mix rich, prop full
Pump=As req
--Climb--
Clear Rwy=L/G up, strobes off
Abv 800'=Flaps up, taxi lht & pump off
Pwr=2500rpm, lean mix thru clb
Oil Temp & CHT=240f/116c & 500f/260c max
Fuel=Balanced
Oxy=As req
--Cruise--
Pwr=Prop 2400rpm, lean mix
Fuel/Instr/Nav=Monitor
CHT=Blw 435f/224c max cruise, 400f/205c econ cruise
--Descent--
Fuel=Balanced
Altimeters=Set
Mix=Enrichen thru descent
CHT=Maintain abv 180f/82c
Oxy=Off
--Pre Landing--
Fuel=Fullest tank
Pwr=Mix rich, prop high
L/G=Dwn (140kts max), 3 green
Flaps=Full (120kts max)
Taxi/Strobes=On
--Balked Landing--
Throttle=Full
Airspeed=95kts, establish clb
Flaps=Retract 20deg
Positive Rate=L/G up
--Taxi In--
Pwr=Throttle idle, lean mix
Time In=Note
Flaps=Up
Trim=Set t/o
Door Seal, Elec Trim, Lndg/Strobes=Off
Xpdr=On
--Shutdown--
Xpdr/Radios/Avionics=Off
Pwr=Throttle/Mix idle
Fuel/Mags/Lhts/Alt/Batt=Off
Covers/Chocks=Secure
